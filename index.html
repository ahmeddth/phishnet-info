How It Works

From inbox to verdict in seconds

PhishNet connects to your Gmail or Outlook inbox and automatically scans every email through a multi-layer AI pipeline.

1

Connect Email

Sign in securely with Google or Microsoft OAuth. PhishNet never stores your password.

2

Auto-Scan

Every email is automatically routed to the right AI engine based on its content type.

3

AI Analysis

Multiple specialized models analyze text, URLs, attachments, images and QR codes in parallel.

4

Instant Verdict

Each email is labeled Safe ✅, Suspicious ⚠️, or Phishing 🚨 with a confidence score.

AI Engines

Four specialized models.
One unified verdict.

Each engine is purpose-built for its domain, trained on real phishing datasets and integrated into a single orchestration layer.

🧠

Text & NLP Engine

Analyzes email body, subject lines, and sender patterns using transformer-based NLP models trained on phishing corpora. Detects urgency cues, impersonation language, and social engineering patterns.

NLP Transformers Email Body Sender Analysis
🔗

URL & QR Engine

Scans all hyperlinks and decodes QR codes embedded in images. Uses machine learning to classify URLs as safe or malicious based on domain age, lexical patterns, and reputation feeds.

URL Analysis QR Decoding ML Classification Domain Intel
🖼️

Image & Deepfake Engine

Uses OCR to extract and analyze text from screenshots and images. Detects deepfake videos using frame-level analysis to identify AI-generated faces used in video phishing attacks.

OCR Deepfake Detection Computer Vision Frame Analysis
📁

File & Malware Engine

Scans email attachments for malicious indicators including entropy analysis, suspicious file extensions, MIME type mismatches, and AI-based content classification of PDFs and executables.

File Scanning Entropy Analysis MIME Detection AI Classification
📞

Live Call Detection NEW

PhishNet can join live Zoom/Teams meetings via an AI bot, transcribe audio in real time using Whisper, and flag phishing language as it's spoken — protecting users from vishing attacks mid-call.

Recall.ai Whisper ASR Real-time NLP
